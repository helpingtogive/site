We are building the next generation of betting and gaming technology. If you are passionate about solving complex problems, we would love to hear from you.

Open Positions
Android Developer
Full-time 🇬🇧 Remote / Derby Mid–Senior

We are looking for an experienced Android Developer to join our mobile engineering team. You will be building native Android applications for betting and gaming operators — handling real-time data, complex state management, and delivering reliable experiences across the Android ecosystem.

  • 3+ years of commercial Android development experience
  • Strong proficiency in Kotlin; experience with Jetpack Compose is a plus
  • Understanding of MVVM/MVI architecture patterns
  • Experience with REST APIs, WebSockets, and real-time data handling
  • Knowledge of Google Play publishing policies and in-app billing
  • Bonus: experience in regulated industries (fintech, gaming, healthcare)
QA Engineer
Full-time 🇬🇧 Remote / Derby Mid-level

Join our QA team to ensure the quality and stability of our mobile and web platforms. You will design test plans, automate regression suites, and work closely with developers to catch issues before they reach production.

  • 2+ years in software QA in an agile environment
  • Experience with automated testing frameworks (Appium, Espresso, XCTest)
  • Proficiency in writing and maintaining test documentation
  • Familiarity with CI/CD pipelines (GitHub Actions, Bitrise, or similar)
  • Strong analytical thinking and an eye for edge cases
  • Experience testing payment flows or regulated financial products is a plus
Data Analyst
Full-time 🇬🇧 Remote / Derby Mid–Senior

We are seeking a Data Analyst to help our clients understand player behaviour, operational performance, and risk signals. You will work with large datasets, build dashboards, and surface actionable insights for product and operations teams.

  • 3+ years of experience in data analysis or business intelligence
  • Proficiency in SQL; Python or R is strongly preferred
  • Experience with BI tools (Tableau, Looker, or Metabase)
  • Ability to communicate insights clearly to non-technical stakeholders
  • Understanding of statistical modelling and cohort analysis
  • Domain knowledge in gaming, fintech, or e-commerce analytics is a strong plus
