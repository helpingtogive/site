Our Commitment

Responsible Gaming

We believe that entertainment should always be safe. Every platform and product we create is built with player protection at its foundation.

Responsible gaming is not a regulatory checkbox for Helping to Give — it is a core design principle. We work with operators to embed player safety tools directly into the product experience, making responsible choices the default rather than the exception.

Our Principles
01

Player Identity Verification

All platforms we build include robust KYC (Know Your Customer) flows. Age verification is mandatory at registration, ensuring that gambling products remain inaccessible to minors. We integrate industry-leading identity verification providers to streamline the process without compromising security.

02

Deposit & Spend Limits

Our systems allow players to set daily, weekly, and monthly deposit limits at any time. Once a limit is set, it cannot be increased immediately — a mandatory cooling-off period is enforced to prevent impulsive decisions. Decreasing limits takes effect immediately.

03

Self-Exclusion & Time-Out

We implement compliant self-exclusion tools that connect with the GAMSTOP national self-exclusion scheme in the UK. Players can also set temporary time-outs ranging from 24 hours to 6 weeks, giving them control over their gaming habits.

04

Behaviour Monitoring & Early Intervention

Our AI-driven analytics detect patterns associated with problem gambling — rapid escalation of bets, unusual session lengths, and loss-chasing behaviour. When risk indicators are flagged, the system prompts responsible gaming messages and notifies the operator's safeguarding team.

05

Transparent Communication

Every product we deliver includes clear, plain-language communication about the risks of gambling. Odds are displayed honestly, and promotional materials never obscure terms and conditions.

06

Regulatory Alignment

We continuously monitor guidelines from the UK Gambling Commission, GamCare, and GambleAware to ensure our implementations remain at the forefront of responsible gaming best practice. Compliance is built into our development lifecycle from the first sprint.

Need Support?

If you or someone you know is struggling with gambling, please reach out to one of the following organisations. Help is available 24 hours a day.

🆘 GamCare — Free support for gambling problems 🛡️ BeGambleAware — Advice and support ⛔ GAMSTOP — National self-exclusion scheme
