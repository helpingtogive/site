About the Company

Who We Are

A team of technologists, compliance specialists, and product builders dedicated to elevating the global betting and gaming industry.

Our Story
Born from Expertise, Built for Scale

Helping to Give Limited was established in the United Kingdom with a clear ambition: to bring enterprise-grade technology and transparent operational practices to betting and gaming businesses worldwide.

Our leadership brings together backgrounds in fintech, software engineering, product design, and regulatory law. This rare combination allows us to deliver solutions that are not only technically excellent but genuinely compliant with the evolving landscape of gaming regulation.

Registered in Derby, England, we operate under the oversight of UK regulatory authorities and take seriously our responsibility to promote safe, fair, and responsible gaming.
