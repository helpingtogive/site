Our Services

End-to-end technology and consulting services for betting and gaming operators — from inception to launch to ongoing optimisation.

🍎

iOS App Development

We design and develop native iOS applications for betting and gaming platforms — optimised for speed, security, and App Store compliance. Our iOS engineers work with Swift and SwiftUI to deliver fluid, responsive experiences on iPhone and iPad.

From account management and live betting streams to payment integrations and biometric authentication, we build every feature with user retention in mind.

Swift / SwiftUI App Store Optimisation Push Notifications Biometric Auth Real-time Data
🤖

Android App Development

Native Android applications built with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose, designed for the full diversity of Android devices. We ensure compatibility across screen sizes, OS versions, and hardware configurations while maintaining high performance.

Our Android team has deep experience navigating Google Play policies in regulated industries, ensuring your application meets compliance requirements at submission.

Kotlin / Jetpack Compose Google Play Compliance Material Design 3 In-App Payments Offline Mode
🎨

App Design

UX/UI design that balances conversion optimisation with responsible gaming principles. We conduct user research, prototype rapidly, and deliver polished design systems that scale across mobile and web.

Every interface we design is accessibility-tested and aligned with the visual identity of your brand. We deliver Figma components ready for direct handoff to development.

Figma Design Systems UX Research Prototyping Accessibility (WCAG 2.1) Brand Integration
📈

App Promotion

Data-driven user acquisition strategies tailored for regulated markets. We design and run campaigns across programmatic channels, affiliate networks, and SEO — always within the bounds of advertising standards for gambling operators.

Our analytics team tracks every conversion touchpoint, enabling you to optimise spend, reduce CAC, and grow your player base sustainably.

ASO (App Store Optimisation) Affiliate Marketing Programmatic Ads Retention Analytics Responsible Advertising
⚖️

Compliance Consulting

We monitor legislative changes across key jurisdictions and translate regulatory requirements into clear product and operational directives. Our compliance practice covers UK Gambling Commission licensing, GDPR, AML procedures, and responsible gaming obligations.

Whether you are applying for a new licence or auditing an existing operation, our team ensures you are fully prepared for regulatory scrutiny.

UKGC Licensing GDPR / Data Protection AML Procedures KYC Integration Compliance Audits
